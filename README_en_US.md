# WuCai Plugin for SiYuan

WuCai is a versatile tool that allows you to highlight, annotate, and take notes on any webpage.

### Key Features:
* **Full-Text Clipping:** Save entire articles for offline or "Read It Later" access.
* **Cross-Browser Support:** Fully compatible with Chrome, Edge, Safari, Firefox, Lemur, Kiwi, Rainsee, and more.
* **Multi-Device Sync:** Seamlessly synchronize your data across different browsers and devices.
* **Native Integrations:** Sync your data directly to **Obsidian, SiYuan, Flomo, and Logseq**.
* **Robust API:** Integrated with productivity tools like **uTools, PopClip, and Quicker**.

[Click here](https://doc.wucai.site/) to learn more.

## SiYuan Integration Features
With the WuCai SiYuan plugin, you can effortlessly sync your highlights and annotations to [SiYuan](https://b3log.org/siyuan/). It also supports the synchronization of full-text clippings directly into your SiYuan workspace.
